Tips and Advice

“Sticky Note Weekly Layout Hack”

Author: @bujo_becca

If you’re afraid of committing to a layout, use sticky notes for your tasks! It’s reusable, and you can move things around without ruining your spread.

“Track Your Mood with Simple Doodles”

Author: @zenpages

I started drawing a mini weather icon each day for my mood. Sunny for happy, cloudy for low energy, etc. It keeps me consistent and adds charm!

“No Art Skills? Use Stamps!”

Author: @minimal_mona

If you're not into drawing, rubber stamps and stencils can save time and still give your pages a polished look. I get mine on Etsy and Michaels!

Product Recommendations

“Game-Changer: Crayola SuperTips”

Author: @creative_cleo

They’re super affordable and come in so many colors. Great for lettering, shading, and color coding your lists!

“Stickers from Shein? YES.”

Author: @bujobabe97

Don’t sleep on Shein’s stationery section. I got a huge sticker pack for under $3. Makes every spread feel fresh and personal.

“The Journal I Keep Reordering”

Author: @dottedlines

I swear by the Lemome dotted journal. Thick pages, hardcover, budget-friendly—and no ghosting from my pens!
