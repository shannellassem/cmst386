Responsive Design

What is it?

Responsive design is important because it helps websites look good and work well on any device, like phones, tablets, or computers. People use all kinds of devices to browse the internet, so making sure the site adjusts to different screen sizes makes it easier to use. A responsive website keeps visitors happy and engaged.

Google Analytics

What is it?

Google Analytics offers different reports that help you see what people do on your website.

  1. Audience Reports show who is visiting your website, including their age, location, and devices used. This helps you understand your users better.
  2. Behavior Reports show which pages people visit the most, how long they stay, and if they leave quickly (bounce rate). This helps you know which content is popular or needs improvement.
  3. Acquisition Reports tell you how people find your website, like through Google search, social media, or other websites. This shows which marketing methods are working and where your visitors come from.
