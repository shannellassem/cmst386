Description

Bujo Dream is a passion-driven website that introduces beginners and enthusiasts to the world of bullet journaling. The site features beginner guides, visual spread ideas, printable templates, and content from journal influencers who share their process step-by-step. The goal is to help people plan creatively, manage their goals, express themselves through journaling, to provide guides, aids, and inspiration to any who does bullet journals no matter what skill level they have. Beginners can learn by look through guide magazines while intermediate and experts can look through any bullet journal spread theme inspirations and see what others in the communities have done. The target audience is mostly female users aged 16–35 who enjoy design, mental wellness, productivity, and artistic hobbies. The site will also link where users can get materials for their bullet journal. Most access information through mobile devices, so responsive design is key. Content is written and designed by me, with credited contributions from creators and are any spread inspirations taken from Pinterest. It will be updated monthly with new ideas and guides.

Growth and Maintenance

As Bujo Dream grows, I’ll expand into digital downloads, journaling challenges, and guest blog posts. To make the site easy to maintain, I’ll use reusable page layouts and organize content by category. Monthly updates will include adding tutorials, updating inspiration galleries, and checking all pages for errors, broken links, or outdated info.

Organization

The site will be organized into 10 main pages.

  • The About Us section includes pages like Our Mission, Our Story, Our Values, and Meet the Team.
  • Guides houses Featured topics, Latest articles, a general Search function, and Search by Category.
  • The For Beginners section offers helpful content such as step-by-step video tutorials, basic tips, and recommended supplies.
  • Tutorials expands on that with a full library, advanced techniques, and workshop sign-up pages.
  • Inspiration focuses on seasonal themes, user-submitted spreads, mood boards, and journaling challenges.
  • Merch includes product pages for branded T-shirts, mugs, posters, and journals.
  • Supplies is broken down into budget-friendly options, where to buy, essential vs. add-on supplies, and user reviews.
  • The Community Forum allows users to join a beginners’ group, share spreads, give tips, and recommend products.
  • Contact Us provides a contact form and general contact info.

Security

It will be optional for users to creat an account or not. Creating an account allows users to save any guides, tutorials, or inspiration they find on the site. There will be an e-commerce that allows users to buy merch. So password protection is needed. However, I will ensure security by using HTTPS, keeping images optimized, and not including any unsafe third-party plugins or forms.
